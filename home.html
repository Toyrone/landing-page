After more than a decade of effort, the NHAct was approved by the National Assembly in 2013 and signed into law by the president in 2014.It was gazetted in 2016 but has never been funded. The NHAct stipulates that 1% of consolidated federal revenues be set aside to support the BHCPF. 50% of the funds are allocated to the National Health Insurace Scheme (NHIS) 'gateway', 45% through the NAtional Primary Health Care Development Agency (NPHCDA) 'gateway', and 5% are aside to deal with emergencies.
